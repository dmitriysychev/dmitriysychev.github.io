    My name is Dmitriy Sychev, I was born in Kazakhstan and came to the US in 2016 to pursue my goals. I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin in May 2020 with major in Computer Science and minor in entrepreneurship. During my studies I became a student leader and a mentor for Inventor's Program in the UT Austin, facilitating hackathons and meetings with companies such as ConocoPhilips and Trend Micro. Later I became a TA for one of the Inventor's Pogram Practicum courses focused on Data Science in the energy industry.
    But my personal development did not stop on this, during my junior year in the UT I became more involved in the research, participating in the Undergraduate Research Forum and winning a 1st place and College of Natural Sciences award for excellence and innovation.
